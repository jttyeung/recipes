Ingredients:
 - 2 tsp tahini
 - 1 clove garlic
 - 1 tbsp onion
 - 1/4 cup lemon juice
 - 1/3 cup olive oil
 - honey to taste
 - salt to taste


Directions:
1. Finely chop garlic and onion.
2. Place all measured ingredients into a bowl and mix, adding in honey and salt to taste.
3. Serve with your favorite salad.
